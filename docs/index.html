How The Sweepstake Works

Points System

  • Win = 3 points
  • Draw = 2 points
  • Loss = 0 points

Your team earns points for every match they play in the tournament (group stage and knockouts).

Quiz Bonus

Weekly quiz scores are added to your leaderboard total. The Pts column shows your combined score (match points + quiz points). The Quiz column shows your cumulative quiz score separately.

Tiebreakers

If two or more participants have the same total points, the ranking is determined by:

  1. Goal Difference (GD) — goals scored minus goals conceded (highest first)
  2. Goals Scored (GF) — most goals first
  3. Alphabetical by participant first name

GF, GA, and GD columns are shown on the leaderboard so you can see how tiebreakers apply.

How It’s Updated

Scores are entered into the master spreadsheet after each match day. The website is then regenerated and updated automatically.
